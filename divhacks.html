DivHacks @Columbia University

I participated in a 3 day hackathon with a team of 4. Collectively, my team decided to build a website that connected people and organizations that resonated on similar global issues. We coined our site 'DivHacktivism'. Follow along on the brainstorming and execution process

Meet the team!

Problem Statement

01. A lot of people are passionate about activism and issues.
02. But where do they start?
03. People need a beginner-friendly method of finding nonprofits (npos).

Solution

DivHacktivism: We connect people to nonprofits, bridging the gap between those who want to help but don’t know how and those who need support.

Our Features

Database of 150+ NPOs
Search engine & filtering algorithm
AI-backed recommendation system
Request service for NPOs to join our database
